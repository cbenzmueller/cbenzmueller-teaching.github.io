Special and General Relativity Theory

Filip Tolovski (Freie Universität Berlin)

Special Relativity theory and General Relativity theory from Albert Einstein have since their publication in 1905 and 1915, accordingly, provided answers to the everlasting questions of humanity about the nature of time and the gravitation. In this presentation, adding to classical mechanics, an outline of the Special and General Relativity theory will be given, finishing with a mention of the possibility for time travel according to the General Relativitiy Theory.
