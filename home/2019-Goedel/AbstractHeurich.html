Gödel's Rationalism and Realism

Manuel Heurich (Freie Universität Berlin)

In this first part of the presentation we will take a closer look at the tasks that he attributes to philosophy and how his thoughts can be clustered into the two points of focus realism and rationalism. Gödel's basic philosophical views can be narrowed down to these two streams. Therefore, we will try to structure and examine his main beliefs to get a basic idea of his mindset.
