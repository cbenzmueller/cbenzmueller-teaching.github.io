Kurt Gödel's ›Maximen Philosophie‹. An Overview

Eva-Maria Engelen (Universität Konstanz)

Abstract: The talk offers a review of Gödel's ›Maximen Philosophie‹ within the realms of the state of research. The edition of the 15 philosophical notebooks that have been found in Gödel's Nachlass is an on-going work already in progress, which will be multiannual. The statements made here can therefore be considered to be only applicable for the time being, but a fascinating see behind the curtain can already be opened up.
