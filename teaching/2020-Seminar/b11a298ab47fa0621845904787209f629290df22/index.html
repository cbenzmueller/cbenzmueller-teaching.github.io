Normative Reasoning and Machine Ethics

Seminar (Winter 2019/20)

Prof. Dr. Christoph Benzmüller

Monday, 24 February 2019 - Friday, 28 February 2019

In this seminar participants will survey some ethical challenges to artificial intelligence and discuss possible solutions. A particular focus will be on ethical governor architectures and explicit normative reasoning.
