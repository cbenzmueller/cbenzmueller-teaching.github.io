First German National CLAIRE Meeting
— TRUSTED AI —

Berlin, September 28, 2021



Trusted AI — Unattainable vision or social mission? Opportunity or onerous burden? Such questions are in the focus of the first, constituting meeting of the German chapter of CLAIRE, the Confederation of Laboratories for AI Research in Europe.

The meeting brings CLAIRE members together with representatives from industry, politics and science and and offers all interested parties the opportunity to get in contact with CLAIRE and to find out about CLAIRE's mission.

The one-day event will take place on September 28. in Berlin (or online) as part of KI 2021, the 44th German Conference on Artificial Intelligence.

  • Introduction to CLAIRE (max. 60 min)
