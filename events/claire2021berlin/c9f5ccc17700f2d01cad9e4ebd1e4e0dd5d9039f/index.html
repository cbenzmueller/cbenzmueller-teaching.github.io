German National CLAIRE Meeting
on TRUSTED AI

Berlin, September 27/28, 2021



Trusted AI — Unattainable Vision or Societal Mandate? This and related questions are in the focus of the first German national meeting of CLAIRE, the Confederation of Laboratories for AI Research in Europe. The one-day event is being held on September 27. or 28. in Berlin (and/or online) in affiliation with KI 2021, the 44th German Conference on Artificial Intelligence.

More information to come ...

Tentative Event Structure
  • Morning Session 1
    • (10min) Welcome
    • (20min) CLAIRE vision and activities
    • (20min) Visionary keynote on Trusted AI
    • (25min) Discussion
  • Break
  • Morning Session 2
    • (60min) Invited presentations
      • (10min+10min Discussion) Industry

  • Lunchbreak
  • Afternoon Session 1

  • Afternoon Session 2
