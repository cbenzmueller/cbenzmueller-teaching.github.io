German National CLAIRE Meeting
on TRUSTED AI

Berlin, September 27/28, 2021



Trusted AI -- Luxury Dream or Societal Mandate? This and related questions are in the focus of the first German national meeting of CLAIRE, the Confederation of Laboratories for AI Research in Europe.
The one-day event is being held on September 27. or 28. in Berlin (and/or online) in affiliation with KI 2021, the 44th German Conference on Artificial Intelligence.


Tentative Event Structure
  • Welcome (15 min)
  • Introduction
    • CLAIRE Vision and Activities - CLAIRE Core Team (30min; candidates: Philipp Slussallek, Holger Hoos)
    • CLAIRE Reports from Neighboring Countries (selected short reports, candidates: Urban, ...)
  • Lectures on Trustworthy AI (20 min each)
    • Industry (candidates: ???)
    • Academia AI/Law/Philosophy (candidates: Katharina Zweig, Kaiserslautern, Georg Borges, Saarbruecken)
    • Politics (candidates: member from national ethics council?, Anna Christmann?)
  • Big Invited Talk (45min; candidates: Joanna Bryson, Berlin)
  • Panel Discussion (45min; candidates: all presenters of this event)
  • Activities in Berlin on Trustworthy AI and more (15 min each)
    • Berlin Ethics Lab (candidate: Sabine Ammon)
    • Interdisziplinäre Arbeitsgruppe Verantwortung: Maschinelles Lernen und Künstliche Intelligenz der Berlin-Brandenburgischen Akademie der Wissenschaften (candidate: Isabella Hermann)
    • Dual Use AG at FU Berlin (candidate: David Niebauer)
  • Socializing Event and Networking
